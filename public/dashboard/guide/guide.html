User Guide

Welcome to the User Guide page. Now, going straight to work, Here are the following steps regarding use of the website . We tried to mention the basic rules and regulations as far ar possible.

1) Login/ SignIn:(Home -> Enter -> Login)

Here, just enter your email and password to go to the Dashboard. If you don't have an account, click on the colored "Register" link to be directed to the register page. If you've forgot your password and want to reset it, click on the forgot password link, and you'll be taken to the page where you can recover or change your password.


2) Register/ SignUp:(Home -> Enter -> Register)

You have to enter your details to make an account. Input your official First Name, Last Name, email id, and password. After clicking SignUp you'll be our official subscriber. If already have an account, click on the colored "Login" link to be directed to the login page.


3) Dashboard:(Home -> Enter -> Login -> Dashboard)

This is your dashboard. Here you'll be able to access various tabs and options. On the left is a sidebar where you can go to Home, or visit your profile, download books from Libgen or Report an issue. In the main body are Profile , Course Materials , Your picks, Be a contributor, Request a material and Log Out

--->In the Profile tab, you can view the information about your profile e.g Name, Password and also be able to edit them

--->Your Picks tab lists the documents and files that you've starred and don't want to find through much hassle and scrolling.

--->Want to browse contents of our vast repo? Click on the Course Materials tab.

--->Eager to contribute to our initiative? Click on the Be a Contributor tab, where you'll just have to enter your email id, and click ok , by which your request will reach the admins. Our admins will then judge your capability and if you're eligible, you'll get a confirmation link in your email.
